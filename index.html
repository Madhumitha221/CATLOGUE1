About Ather Energy

Ather Energy is a pioneering Indian electric vehicle manufacturer, founded in 2013. We are committed to designing and building intelligent electric scooters and a robust public charging infrastructure (Ather Grid) to accelerate the adoption of electric vehicles in India. Our mission is to transform urban commute with sustainable and high-performance mobility solutions.

Our flagship products, the Ather 450X and 450S, are known for their cutting-edge technology, superior performance, and connected features, offering a seamless and exhilarating riding experience. We believe in an ecosystem approach, providing comprehensive solutions from vehicle to charging to ownership experience.
